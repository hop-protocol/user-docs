# Why can I not access Hop.exchange?

* Cuba
* Iran
* North Korea
* Syria
* Belarus
* Burundi
* Central African Republic
* Democratic Republic of the Congo
* Lebanon
* Libya
* Nicaragua
* Somalia
* South Sudan
* Sudan and Darfu
* Venezuela
* Yemen
* Zimbabwe
* Ethiopia
* Myanmar
* Burundi
* Liberia
* Côte d’Ivoire
